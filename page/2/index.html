About me

Sun, Jan 4, 2015 Hi there, stranger! My name is Gianguido Sorà, alias peppelakappa/plk/gsora and I’m a young italian computer science student. I don’t really like to give myself a tag when talking of software development, instead I like to introduce myself as a young hacker where “hacker” means someone who likes to tinker with new stuff and learn how it works, then have fun modifying it! Even if it’s not a great practice for someone who works with programming languages to declare a preferred one, my love for Python must be known, but lately I’m starting to have a lot of fun with Golang too.
